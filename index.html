About Me

Hey there! I'm Victor — a Research Scientist working in Computer Vision and Deep Learning. I’ve been passionate about image and video generation since 2018, when I first got hands-on with GANs during an internship. Since then, I’ve followed the exciting evolution of the field, diving into diffusion models, MaskGIT, and autoregressive techniques. My research has led to publications at top conferences like CVPR, ICCV, and ICLR, and I enjoy contributing to open-source projects. I’m especially interested in building models that work in real-time, particularly for safety-critical applications like autonomous driving.
